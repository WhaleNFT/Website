Creation Story

On April 1st, the day I decided to create my project on the TON blockchain, I chose to create the Whale NFT collection as my business card - a set of unique digital assets dedicated to majestic whales. Inspired by the beauty and power of these sea creatures, I began work on the collection. My passion for cryptocurrencies and art inspired me to create each unique whale, reflecting the diversity of human existence. I invested a piece of my soul into each image, striving to convey in them all the power and grandeur of these marine giants. Whale NFT is not only an expression of my love for whales and art but also a unique way to share this passion with others. Each NFT became a kind of immersion into the world of the sea and art, which can be appreciated and collected.
